This Bible contradiction is from the Skeptic's Annotated Bible.

Verses

Judge not.

Matthew 7:1 View context

Judge not, that ye be not judged.

Luke 6:37 View context

Judge not, and ye shall not be judged: condemn not, and ye shall not be condemned: forgive, and ye shall be forgiven:

Romans 2:1 View context

Therefore thou art inexcusable, O man, whosoever thou art that judgest: for wherein thou judgest another, thou condemnest thyself; for thou that judgest doest the same things.

Romans 14:10 View context

But why dost thou judge thy brother? or why dost thou set at nought thy brother? for we shall all stand before the judgment seat of Christ.

James 4:12 View context

There is one lawgiver, who is able to save and to destroy: who art thou that judgest another?

Judge everything and everyone.

Leviticus 19:15 View context

Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the poor, nor honour the person of the mighty: [but] in righteousness shalt thou judge thy neighbour.

John 7:24 View context

Judge not according to the appearance, but judge righteous judgment.

1 Corinthians 2:15 View context

But he that is spiritual judgeth all things, yet he himself is judged of no man.

1 Corinthians 5:12-13 View context

For what have I to do to judge them also that are without? do not ye judge them that are within?

But them that are without God judgeth. Therefore put away from among yourselves that wicked person.

1 Corinthians 6:2-3 View context

Do ye not know that the saints shall judge the world? and if the world shall be judged by you, are ye unworthy to judge the smallest matters?

Know ye not that we shall judge angels? how much more things that pertain to this life?
