Which flying creeping things may we eat? View all View original

This Bible contradiction is from the Skeptic's Annotated Bible.

Verses

All are unclean and may not be eaten.

Deuteronomy 14:19 View context

And every creeping thing that flieth [is] unclean unto you: they shall not be eaten.

Some may be eaten.

Leviticus 11:21-23 View context

Yet these may ye eat of every flying creeping thing that goeth upon [all] four, which have legs above their feet, to leap withal upon the earth;

[Even] these of them ye may eat; the locust after his kind, and the bald locust after his kind, and the beetle after his kind, and the grasshopper after his kind.

But all [other] flying creeping things, which have four feet, [shall be] an abomination unto you.
