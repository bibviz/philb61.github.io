Who raised Jesus from the dead? View all View original

This Bible contradiction is from the Skeptic's Annotated Bible.

Verses

Jesus raised himself from the dead.

John 2:19-21 View context

Jesus answered and said unto them, Destroy this temple, and in three days I will raise it up.

Then said the Jews, Forty and six years was this temple in building, and wilt thou rear it up in three days?

But he spake of the temple of his body.

God raised him from the dead.

Acts 2:24 View context

Whom God hath raised up, having loosed the pains of death: because it was not possible that he should be holden of it.

Acts 4:10 View context

Be it known unto you all, and to all the people of Israel, that by the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth, whom ye crucified, whom God raised from the dead, [even] by him doth this man stand here before you whole.

Acts 13:30 View context

But God raised him from the dead:

Galatians 1:1 View context

Paul, an apostle, (not of men, neither by man, but by Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead;)

Colossians 2:12 View context

Buried with him in baptism, wherein also ye are risen with [him] through the faith of the operation of God, who hath raised him from the dead.

1 Thessalonians 1:10 View context

And to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, [even] Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come.
